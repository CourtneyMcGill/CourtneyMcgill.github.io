Course Scheduler

  • Senior Project at Colgate University
  • Legacy course selection system required students to look up clases in a course catalogue, map out their schedule by hand, and then enter course codes into a seperate registration platform
  • Our solution allowed students to browse for courses and directly add them to a digital calender to vizualize potential schedules. When they were satistifed with their schedule, they could register their selections in the same interface.

TrackHack

  • Hackathon project from HackMIT 2014
  • One stop playlist platform
  • Spotify, SoundCloud, and Youtube all host different songs and artists- TrackHack allows you to create playlists containing songs from multiple providers and stream them all in one place.
